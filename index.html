About

Hi there! 😁

Welcome to my webpage, home of very little! My name is Jesse Mills, I'm a software developer based in the NYC area.

I write code primarily in Java, Scala, and Javascript, but dabble in Ruby & C# as well. These days I am the Development Team Lead for H.Bloom, leading their efforts around floral and plant orders. 🌸🌹💐🌸💐🌹🌸

Prior to my current role, I was the product manager for a sports technology company called BSports, which was originally part of Bloomberg LP, before it was eventually acquired by STATS LLC. In that role I led efforts around our line of baseball products targeting MLB, international, and media clients.

In addition to my professional roles, I serve on the immigrant youth ally board for Atlas:DIY a non-profit based in Sunset Park, Brooklyn which supports immigrant youth empowerment. Check them out they do really valuable and important work!

I also worked as a TA for General Assembly, participated in the Civic Hall Labs Pro Bono Tech program, volunteered my time for an immigrant service center in Queens, and prior to moving to NYC worked for the Miami Marlins and Tampa Bay Rays
